Privacy Policy

Sir Tips-A-Lot

Effective Date: December 21, 2024

Summary: Sir Tips-A-Lot does not collect, store, or share any personal data. Your privacy is completely protected.

Data Collection

Sir Tips-A-Lot is designed with your privacy in mind. The app:

  • Does not collect any personal information
  • Does not require an account or login
  • Does not use analytics or tracking
  • Does not access the internet
  • Does not store your calculations

How the App Works

All tip calculations are performed locally on your device. The numbers you enter are processed in real-time and are never saved or transmitted anywhere. When you close the app, all data is gone.

Third-Party Services

This app does not integrate with any third-party services, APIs, or SDKs that would collect your data. The optional "Tip Jar" feature uses external payment links (Venmo, Cash App, PayPal) which open in Safari and are subject to their respective privacy policies.

Children's Privacy

Our app does not collect any information from anyone, including children under the age of 13.

Changes to This Policy

If we ever update this privacy policy, we will post the new policy on this page with an updated effective date.
