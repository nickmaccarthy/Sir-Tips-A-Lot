Frequently Asked Questions

How do I calculate a tip?

Simply enter your bill amount and tap one of the preset tip percentages (18%, 20%, 25%) or use the custom option to enter any percentage you'd like.

How do I split the bill?

Use the "Split Between" option and tap + or - to adjust the number of people. The app will show you each person's share.

What does "Round Up Tip" do?

When enabled, this rounds your tip up to the nearest whole dollar for easier payment.

Is my data private?

Absolutely! Sir Tips-A-Lot doesn't collect, store, or share any data. All calculations happen locally on your device. See our Privacy Policy for details.

How do I report a bug?

Please email us at nickmaccarthy@gmail.com with a description of the issue and your iOS version.
